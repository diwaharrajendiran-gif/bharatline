About BharatLine

Keeping India Informed, Every Minute

BharatLine is a free, independent Indian news aggregator that brings together the latest headlines from India's most trusted news publishers — all in one place, in real time.

We believe every Indian deserves fast, unbiased access to news — whether it's politics, business, cricket, entertainment, or technology. Instead of hopping between ten different news websites, BharatLine collects them for you automatically using public RSS feeds.

What We Do

BharatLine aggregates publicly available RSS feeds from leading Indian news sources including NDTV, Times of India, The Hindu, Hindustan Times, Economic Times, Mint, Indian Express, India Today, Bollywood Hungama, Deccan Herald, New Indian Express, and more.

We do not write, edit, or modify any news content. All articles link directly back to the original publisher's website. BharatLine is purely a discovery and aggregation platform.
